Who I Am

Entrepreneur who learned to code.
Developer who never forgot GTM.

I’m Matt Cretzman — entrepreneur, fractional CMO, AI systems builder, and full-stack AI developer. Eight ventures. One stack. Unlimited urgency.

Before any of this worked, I cleaned pools, flipped houses, failed at real estate, and tried insurance. From 2016 to 2019, nothing stuck — and I had two kids watching. In 2019, I met Brad, the founder of a marketing agency in Keller, who gave me a hand up at the right moment. That conversation changed everything.

In January 2020, I launched Stormbreaker Digital. What started as a LinkedIn growth agency became something I couldn’t have predicted: an AI agent systems company. Then I became something I also couldn’t have predicted: a full-stack developer.

I’m a Claude power user — Claude Code, Claude Projects, custom Connectors, AI Skills. I build in Cursor, deploy on Vercel, and orchestrate everything through OpenClaw. I went from marketer to someone who ships PWAs, Zoom apps, and mobile apps without a dev team. Each venture built faster than the one before, because the stack keeps getting sharper.

I also wrote a book on LinkedIn with Tony Jeary. Named a children’s home after my daughter Azlynn. Some things you build for business. Some things you build because you have to.

Method

How I build

Seven years of GTM experience in the trenches. Then AI unlocked something I didn’t see coming.

Claude

The Engine

Claude Code for production apps. Claude Projects across every venture. Custom Connectors — I’ve built my own. AI Skills were the original inspiration behind Skill Refinery. I push Claude further than most people know is possible.

Cursor + Vercel

Write & Ship

Cursor is where code gets written. Vercel is where it deploys — dozens of live PWAs, Zoom apps, mobile apps, blogs, and SaaS tools. Full-stack without a dev team and without a CS degree.

OpenClaw

The Middleware

OpenClaw connects Claude, MCP, APIs, and every external tool into autonomous agent systems. Researchers, writers, SDRs, publishers, analysts — each runs on a schedule, feeds the next, compounds over time.

7 Years GTM

The Foundation

Before the AI stack existed, I was doing the work manually — outbound, LinkedIn campaigns, positioning, pipeline. That’s what makes the systems actually convert. You can’t automate a motion you don’t understand.

“What makes this unusual: I see software with a business owner’s eyes. I know which features move revenue. I know what copy should say before I write the UI that displays it. Most developers don’t think like that. Most marketers can’t build what they’re thinking. I do both — and I move with the speed and urgency of someone whose livelihood depends on shipping. Because it does.”
