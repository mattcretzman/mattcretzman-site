The Build Story

Started as a LinkedIn agency.
Became something bigger.

Stormbreaker started as a LinkedIn growth agency in January 2020. The early thesis was straightforward: B2B companies were leaving pipeline on the table because their leaders didn’t know how to use LinkedIn effectively. We fixed that.

For the first few years, I was doing the work most agencies outsource or under-deliver on — personally running outbound campaigns, building prospect lists, writing sequences, coaching clients on positioning, and iterating until the pipeline moved. Seven years of that across dozens of companies and every B2B vertical imaginable. That depth is what most agencies don’t have, because most agency founders were never operators first.

What I didn’t plan for was what happened when I started deploying AI agents to do the work we were doing manually. The first autonomous content engine I ran for a client produced more output in a week than a human writer could in a month — at a fraction of the cost. That changed the company.

We stopped being a LinkedIn agency. We became an AI agent systems company. But the GTM expertise stayed — baked into every system we build. We’re not just automating tasks. We’re automating motions we know work because we’ve run them manually for years.
