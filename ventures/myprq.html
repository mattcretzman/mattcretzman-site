The Build Story

The co-author of the standard
had a problem software couldn’t solve.

The co-author of the parliamentary procedure standard used by 23 million North American organizations reached out because he had a real problem that software had never solved: managing who gets to speak, in what order, with what time constraints, in formal meetings that can have hundreds of participants.

Existing tools didn’t understand parliamentary procedure. Generic queue software didn’t have the rule-based logic that formal meetings require. He needed something built by someone who understood the domain. We built it together.

What We Built

Rules-based. Auditable.
Built to the standard.

Parliamentary Logic

Rules-based logic enforcing parliamentary procedure. Time limits, point-of-order handling, and recognition queue all managed in one interface.

Auditable Record

Every recognition tracked. No more he-said-she-said about who was next. Clean, auditable record for any assembly.

Scale to Large Assemblies

HOAs, city councils, boards, fraternal organizations, unions — anywhere formal procedure is required.

Built With Credibility

Co-developed with the person who wrote the standard. Not approximated from the outside — built from the source.
